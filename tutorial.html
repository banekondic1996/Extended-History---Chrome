You're all set! Here are a few tips to get the most out of Extended History.

🗂️
Right-click "Tab Storage" in popup to view active tabs
In the popup, right-click the "Tab Storage" to see all your currently opened tabs. Click any tab in the list to store it for later opening — perfect for clearing clutter without losing pages and lowers ram usage.
📋
Right-click "Sessions" to export
In the popup, right-click the Sessions button to instantly download your current session as a portable HTML file with all your open tabs. Perfect if you don't trust local storage
✅
Hold an item to bulk-select & delete
In the popup, hold (long-press) any Recent History item to enter selection mode. Tap more items to select them, then hit Delete to remove them.
🔍
Full-text search across all history
Use the search bar to find any page by title, URL, or domain. Filter further by date or hour using the pill bar at the top of the history page. Multiple words contained search is performed when space is used, order of words isn't improtant
